1. Strain Profile — What You're Actually Growing

Citron Givré is a phenotype selection from T.H. Seeds' Birthday Cake Kush line — a cross of Birthday Cake (Girl Scout Cookies × Cherry Pie) and a proprietary Kush male. The name translates from French as "frosted lemon," describing the heavy trichome coverage and citrus-forward terpene profile this particular phenotype expresses.

2. Equipment — What 108W Delivers

Two Sanlight STIXX 50 bars produce ~270 µmol/s total PPF at ~2.5 µmol/J. In the HS80's reflective interior at 25–30cm height, that's roughly 350–420 µmol/m²/s average PPFD across the 80×80cm footprint. During a 12-hour flower cycle, DLI ≈ 15–18 mol/m²/day — about 45% of the theoretical optimum where yield plateaus.

Light pipeline from wall watts to realistic yield. Training and canopy management are critical at this wattage — every wasted photon hurts proportionally more than at 300W+.

3. Height Budget — 178cm Breakdown

Side view height budget. With 78cm usable, flip when canopy reaches 25–35cm. At 50–80% stretch, a 35cm canopy becomes 53–63cm final — right at the edge. Flip earlier rather than later.
Flip trigger: With only 78cm of plant zone, flip at 25–35cm canopy height. Indica-dominant genetics stretch 50–80%, so a 35cm canopy at flip becomes ~53–63cm final. You can raise the light, but you can't shrink the plant.

4. Training — Single-Plant ScrOG

One plant filling 80×80cm requires 6–8 weeks of veg (not 4–5 as in multi-plant setups). The clone has been topped once — you're working with at least two main branches that become your canopy framework. Goal: 30–50 evenly spaced bud sites across a flat canopy.

Training progression. With one plant, the net takes 6–8 weeks to fill — be patient. Every empty square at flip wastes photons from your 108W.

5. Clone Backup — Non-Negotiable

TAKE CUTTINGS BEFORE FLIPPING: Take 2–4 cuttings from lower branches during veg weeks 5–7 (lollipop material = free clones). Cut 10–15cm at 45° below a node, scrape stem 2cm, dip in Clonex or honey, place in jiffy pellet or water glass with cut PET bottle dome. 80%+ RH, 22–25°C, 7–14 days to root. Keep rooted clones in 1L pots under 18/6 light. Two healthy rooted clones = genetic insurance. No exceptions before flipping.

Devil's advocate — CalMag under LED: Non-negotiable. LEDs drive faster photosynthesis with less radiant heat, reducing passive calcium uptake. Even in organic soil, LED-grown plants frequently show mid-flower calcium deficiency. Start at 0.5 ml/L week 3, increase to 1 ml/L during peak veg/early flower. A €5 bottle prevents 10–30% yield loss.

9. Harvest — Trichomes, Not Calendar

Check calyxes (bud pods), not sugar leaves. 60× jeweler's loupe. Start checking at flower week 6. Cut just before lights-on — THC peaks during dark period.

10. Drying & Curing

The tent becomes your drying chamber. Remove LEDs. Hang whole branches from the crossbar. Monkey Fan on lowest — never pointed at buds.
