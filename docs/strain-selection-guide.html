Critical for functional use

High Myrcene is the primary driver of couch-lock. Prioritise strains where Caryophyllene + Limonene rank above Myrcene in the terpene profile. When selecting phenotypes, choose peppery-lemon over earthy-musky.

Mokum’s Tulip delivers the only three-pathway anxiolytic profile of any strain here: Linalool (GABA/5-HT), Caryophyllene (CB2), and Limonene (dopamine) all operating at meaningful levels simultaneously. Dutch Passion has published quantified lab data confirming all three terpenes, making this one of only two strains with rigorous independent verification. The Linalool dominance is pharmacologically unique — it operates on serotonin receptors at the same sites targeted by SSRIs, producing pronounced anxiolysis. The caveat is architectural: Dutch Passion explicitly warns that branches are extremely thick and rigid, resisting the bending required for ScrOG. Consider SOG instead for this cultivar.

Blue Gelato 41 holds a unique position in this selection: it is the only strain where α-Pinene ranks as the primary terpene, making it the most directly relevant option for memory retention and focus. α-Pinene is an acetylcholinesterase inhibitor — it protects acetylcholine, the neurotransmitter responsible for memory and concentration, and directly counteracts the short-term memory impairment associated with THC. The Blueberry heritage contributes the fruity complexity; Thin Mint GSC injects Caryophyllene-heavy resin; Sunset Sherbet rounds the profile. The challenge is architectural: at 110–150 cm, this is the tallest option on the list and will require early topping plus aggressive ScrOG management to fit the 80×80 tent.

How to choose based on your priorities: grow management ability, terpene certainty, or anxiolytic intensity.

Easiest Wins for Your Setup

Wedding Gelato, Blue Gem, and AK-47 combine the best terpene profiles with the most manageable growth in an 80×80 cm space under 100W. Tier 3 — Anxiety Relief Priority

When Calm Matters More Than Peak Output

Mokum’s Tulip’s Linalool dominance makes it pharmacologically the strongest anxiolytic on this list (Linalool acts on serotonin receptors at the same sites as SSRIs). Cream Caramel’s extreme phenotype stability eliminates lottery risk. Both carry more sedation risk — micro-dosing is key for functional use.

Mokum’s Tulip Cream Caramel Royal Cookies Super Lemon Haze
Devil’s Advocate — Terpene Data Honesty

Only AK-47 and Super Lemon Haze have truly independent, quantified terpene data from third-party labs. The rest rely on breeder claims or aggregated community reports. Terpene expression varies significantly by phenotype, growing conditions, harvest timing, and cure — the same genetics can produce different ratios from seed to seed. Treat these profiles as probable ranges, not guarantees. Your best insurance is growing multiple seeds and selecting for the aroma profile you want: peppery + citrus = Caryophyllene + Limonene dominant; earthy + musky = Myrcene dominant.
