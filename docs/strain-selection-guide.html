Why These Terpenes?

Your preferred strains and reported effect preferences consistently point to a β-Caryophyllene + Limonene dominant profile — spicy/peppery warmth plus citrus brightness. β-Caryophyllene is the only terpene that acts as a direct CB2 receptor agonist (anti-inflammatory, anxiolytic, physically relaxing without sedation). Limonene adds mood elevation and anxiolytic effects. Together, they create the "relaxing but alert" experience you're after.

Primary Target

Fresh/piney (terpinolene) or floral (linalool). Add complexity. Present in some selections.

Devil's Advocate — The Real Selection Criterion — At 108W in an 80×80cm tent on a first indoor grow, the most important strain characteristic is forgiveness, not terpene perfection. Super Skunk, White Widow, and Northern Lights can all survive suboptimal conditions and still produce decent flower. Gelato and Amnesia Haze punish mistakes much harder. Your terpene preferences point to Tier 1 strains, but your experience level suggests starting with the most forgiving option and getting one successful harvest under your belt before reaching for more demanding genetics.
